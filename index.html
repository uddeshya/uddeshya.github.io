Keywords: OpenCV, Python, Image processing, Machine learning


A publishable research oriented project on "a person's manner of walking" i.e its gait analysis, useful in the field of Advanced Security, Fitness and Medicinal Advancement.

Summer Research Internship

Location: Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati

Keywords: Matlab, Simulink, Power System, Algorithms

Modeling of distribution network and its electromagnetic transient and steady state analysis using Forward-Backward Iterative Technique. This analysis is carried out on a 6-Bus RDN of the Jail station, Guwahati city and a Sample 9-Bus RDN.
A generalized code was generated to carry forward the load flow analysis and electromagnetic transient analysis for the distribution network.
